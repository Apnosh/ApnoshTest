Why most business video falls flat

📷
Low production quality
Shaky phone footage with bad lighting and no editing makes your brand look amateur. Viewers scroll past in under a second. First impressions happen fast and bad video makes a lasting negative one.
💬
No hook or structure
Most business videos ramble without a clear hook, narrative arc, or call to action. The algorithm buries content with low watch time — and viewers won’t watch what doesn’t grab them in the first two seconds.
🎨
Ignoring trends and formats
TikTok and Reels algorithms reward content that uses trending formats, sounds, and editing styles. Posting generic product clips while competitors ride trends means your content never reaches new audiences.
💰
Ad creative that burns budget
Running paid ads with weak video creative is the fastest way to waste money. Ad creative is the number one factor in paid social performance — yet most businesses treat it as an afterthought.
