Why most small business websites don’t convert

🚧
Template sites that all look the same
Drag-and-drop builders produce generic sites that don’t reflect your brand or differentiate you from competitors. Visitors can tell — and they leave.
🐢
Slow load times that kill conversions
Every extra second of load time costs you 7% in conversions. Bloated themes and cheap hosting make your site a revenue leak.
📱
Not optimized for mobile
Over 60% of traffic is mobile, yet most small business sites look broken on phones. If your site doesn’t work on mobile, you’re losing the majority of visitors.
🔍
Invisible to search engines
Without proper SEO structure — meta tags, schema markup, fast load times, internal linking — Google can’t find you. No traffic means no customers.
