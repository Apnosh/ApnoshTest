Common questions

Everything you want to know about creative production

We handle all the planning. After the creative brief, we provide a shot list and preparation checklist tailored to your shoot type. For product shoots, have your items clean and ready. For lifestyle shoots, we coordinate wardrobe, props, and locations. You just show up and we take care of the rest.
We use professional-grade cameras, lenses, lighting kits, stabilizers, and audio equipment. Specific gear depends on the project — product shoots use studio lighting setups, events use fast prime lenses and on-camera lighting, and video shoots include cinema cameras with professional audio capture. All equipment is included in the hourly rate.
It depends on the shoot type and duration. A typical 2-hour product shoot delivers 40–80 edited images. A 2-hour lifestyle shoot delivers 60–120 images. For video, a 2-hour shoot typically yields 1–3 minutes of finished, edited content (or more for basic edits). We set clear deliverable expectations during the creative brief.
Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days for photography and 7–10 business days for video editing. Basic edits can be faster. Rush delivery is available on request for an additional fee. Event highlight reels and social teasers can often be turned around within 48–72 hours.
