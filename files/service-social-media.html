The real reasons most businesses fail on social

🕙
No time to post consistently
You start strong, post for two weeks, then life gets in the way. Inconsistency is worse than not posting at all — it signals to customers that you’re not on top of things.
📷
Content that doesn’t reflect your quality
Quick phone shots and generic captions don’t build trust. Customers judge your business by what they see on social before they ever visit or call.
🔌
No strategy — just posting for the sake of it
Posting without a strategy doesn’t grow an audience or drive customers. Every post needs a purpose — whether that’s reach, trust, or driving action.
📢
Not showing up where your customers actually are
54% of people discover new businesses on social media. If you’re inactive, every one of those potential customers finds your competitor instead.
