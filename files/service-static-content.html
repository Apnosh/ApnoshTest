Why DIY graphics fall flat

📷
Stories that look like afterthoughts
Quick phone screenshots with text slapped on top don’t drive swipe-ups or build brand recognition. Your stories compete with polished brands — and lose when they look unfinished.
📚
Carousels that nobody swipes through
Without a compelling visual hook on slide one and a clear narrative arc, people scroll past. A carousel that doesn’t earn the swipe is worse than not posting at all — it tanks your engagement rate.
📈
Data visuals that confuse instead of impress
Poorly laid-out infographics with cluttered numbers and inconsistent styling undermine your authority. If people can’t understand it at a glance, they won’t save or share it.
🎨
No visual system across formats
When your stories, carousels, and infographics all look like they came from different brands, you lose the cumulative recognition that makes content marketing work.

Single feed posts are standard square or vertical images for your main grid. Story graphics are vertical 9:16 content designed for Instagram and Facebook stories. Carousels are multi-slide posts (up to 10 slides) that users swipe through. Infographics are data-rich visual posts. Each format serves a different strategic purpose and they work best when combined.
Story graphics are $25 each, dropping to $18 each when you order 8 or more per month. Carousel posts are $95 each, dropping to $75 at 4+ per month. Each carousel includes up to 10 custom-designed slides. We storyboard the entire sequence with a compelling hook on slide one, a clear narrative flow, and a strong CTA on the final slide. Most clients find 7–10 slides is the sweet spot for engagement.
Absolutely. You can provide raw data, statistics, research findings, or even a rough outline. We handle all the visual design, data hierarchy, and layout. If you don’t have data ready, we can also research and curate industry-relevant statistics for you.
