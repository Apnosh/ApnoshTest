Why most business feeds don’t convert

📷
DIY graphics that look unpolished
Quick Canva templates and phone photos don’t build the trust needed to convert a profile visitor into a customer. Your feed is your storefront — and most businesses have the digital equivalent of a handwritten cardboard sign.
💬
Captions that get zero engagement
Generic captions like “Happy Monday!” don’t show up in search and don’t encourage interaction. Every caption should be working to grow your reach and drive action.
🎨
No visual consistency across posts
Different fonts, colors, and styles from post to post make your brand look scattered. A cohesive visual identity across your feed signals professionalism and builds recognition.
🔍
Posts invisible to non-followers
Without SEO-optimized captions and strategic hashtags, your posts only reach people who already follow you. The whole point is reaching new potential customers.
